# 1. Getting Started

[← Back to index](README.md) · [Next: Dashboards & Rates →](02-dashboards-and-rates.md)

## Launching the app
Open **Jewelry ERP** from the Start menu (or the desktop icon). The app runs fully offline —
no internet is required except for optional live-rate sync and WhatsApp messaging.

- The window opens to the **login screen** (or the **setup wizard** the very first time).
- All data is stored locally in an encrypted-capable SQLite database on this PC.
- A backup is saved automatically when you close the app (if enabled in Settings).

**Default login:** `admin` / `admin1234` (Admin). Change this after first login.

## First-Time Setup
Only appears the very first time, when no admin account exists yet.

1. **Step 1 — Organization details:** Shop Name, Contact Number, GSTIN (optional, valid
   15-character GSTIN if entered), Billing Address → **Continue**.
2. **Step 2 — Create Super Admin:** Admin Username, Password (min 8 characters),
   Confirm Password → **Finish Setup**.
3. You are logged in automatically as Admin.

## Login, Logout & Screen Lock

**Login**
1. Enter your **Username** and **Password** → **Sign In**.
   - Admins/Managers land on the **MIS Dashboard**; other staff land on the **Dashboard**.
   - Wrong credentials show *"Incorrect username or password."*

**Screen Lock** (protects the till when you step away)
- Click **Lock** in the top header, or the app auto-locks after **15 minutes** of inactivity.
- Type your password on the lock overlay to return to exactly where you were.

**Logout**
- Click **Logout** at the bottom of the left sidebar to end your session.
